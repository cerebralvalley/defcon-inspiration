[Music] This year at Defcon, DARPA and ARPAH are excited to announce the winners of the AI Cyber Challenge and bring a new experience for you to explore. AICC is a 2-year competition to develop automated cyber reasoning systems that can both identify and patch vulnerabilities in code that's critical to our national security and our everyday lives. The first place team will go home with a $4 million prize. the second place team with $3 million and the third place team with $1.5 million. The awards are big because the stakes are high and the challenge is massive. The systems that we all rely on are power plants, hospitals, water systems, banks, and more are pervasively vulnerable to cyber attacks and the current state of cyber security cannot keep up. This year alone, we've seen massive intrusions into our telecom systems, attacks on our grocery stores that have led to supply chain issues and empty shelves, and attacks on hospitals and healthcare providers that impact patient care. The AICC semi-final competition proved that AI systems are capable of not only identifying but also successfully patching real vulnerabilities, so one day we can live in a more cyber secure world. This year we are kicking off by announcing the final competition winners from the Defcon main stage. And we will share competition details and the team's technology with you in the AICC experience all weekend long. Through the new AIC experience, stage talks, and more, you'll learn just how successful these systems are and how you can use them once they're open sourced. After Defcon, AICC teams will open source their software because we are committed to creating innovative technology and transitioning these breakthroughs to the real world. But ultimately, success requires engagement from you. We need partners who deeply understand the stakes of developing and deploying these tools to secure critical infrastructure and beyond. At this year's AIC experience, you'll have the opportunity to learn the stakes of cyber security for five different critical infrastructure sectors. Immerse yourself in the realistic cyber attacks on different sectors and talk to sector experts on challenges and opportunities in their industries. You'll also learn all about the competition, including opportunities to talk with competition organizers and technical experts as they walk you through competition visualizations and answer all your questions. Meet the teams and see their technology in action. Explore competition data, including vulnerabilities and patches found and created by the competitor's systems. Meet the industry partners who have been vital to the competition. Find out how you can take action and support adoption of this technology. And hear more about DARPA and ARPAH's cyber and AI portfolios. Get involved and support the adoption of this game-changing technology. Together, we can create a more secure future for everyone.
