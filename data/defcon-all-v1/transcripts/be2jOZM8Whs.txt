In a network printing, there are two different types of rendering. Client-side and service-side. The rendering, job are handled by Print Driver. Printer driver is an important component of printing. It is the interface component between the OS and the printers. In order to support both client-side and service-side rendering, it is a requirement that the printer drivers are available to both print client and print server. There are two mechanisms for distributing printer driver. They are called Point-And-Print, and Package Point-And-Print.
